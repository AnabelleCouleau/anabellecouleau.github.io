research

Publications & Working Papers

  • Safeguarding the Sip: Drinking Water Quality Regulation, Monitoring and Surveillance in Latin America and the Caribbean IDB Report

    Couleau A., Perez-Urdiales M., and Poli L.

    Inter-American Development Bank · Published April 2026

    A comprehensive regional assessment (260 pages) of drinking water quality regulation and compliance across LAC countries, covering reporting frameworks, monitoring adequacy, and violation patterns.

  • Coffee Futures Prices and Market Momentum Published

    Couleau A., Trujillo-Barrera A., and Etienne X.

    Journal of Commodity Markets · 2025

    Examines momentum dynamics in coffee futures markets and their implications for price discovery and risk management strategies for producers.

  • Weather Shocks and Food Prices in a Very Diverse Country: Evidence from Colombia Submitted

    Almeida A., Bohorquez-Penuela C., Bonilla-Mejia L., and Couleau A.

    Uses granular wholesale price data (SIPSA) to identify the causal impact of extreme rainfall events on food prices across Colombia's heterogeneous geography, documenting four transmission mechanisms from weather shocks to consumer prices.

  • When Does Rural Credit Improve Farm Socio-Ecological Resilience? Heterogeneous Treatment Effects among Colombian Smallholders Working paper

    Zapata-Valencia R.S., Couleau A., and Muñoz-Mora J.C.

    Estimates heterogeneous treatment effects of rural credit access on the socio-ecological resilience of smallholder farms in Colombia, using causal inference methods to identify which farmers benefit most.
